Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to your usage of the software and services provided by Tripstir LLC ("Services"). We only collect your information in order to improve the Services and will never sell or share your information with other legal entities for commercial purposes, except in the cases when it's required as a part of the Services you requested.

The information we collect and how we use it

1. The information you provide

During registration or your use of the Services we ask you to sign in using Facebook so that we can have access to your personal data such as name, current city of residence, friend list, and bio. We use that information to provision the Services, improve the Services, and contact you when necessary. In addition, we store your trip details on our server to provide various services, such as matching your schedule with others that have overlapping trips. All the services can be turned on or off based on your preference.

2. Server logs

Every time you visit the Services from our app, servers log an entry that includes date and time, your IP address, your device unique ID. Whenever you make a request from our app, e.g. create a new trip; we will also log the request as an entry that includes all the request details. We use this information for analytical purposes in aggregated form.

3. Google Maps API(s)

Our Services utilizes Google Maps API(s) to auto-complete the listings of city destinations. As such, users must reference and adhere to the Google Privacy Policy.

Communication privacy

We will keep track of your chat communication in using the Service. However, this information is retained for the sole purpose of providing you with a chat history reference.

Data storage

Services uses third party hosting providers to store your data. We store your account data, contact list, trip data (including comments and likes), and chat data.

Disclosure

Services may disclose personally identifiable information when it's required to comply with law enforcement requests or subpoenas when you violate the Terms of Service.

Changes

This privacy policy may be modified from time to time. When changes are significant, we will update this page as soon as possible.
